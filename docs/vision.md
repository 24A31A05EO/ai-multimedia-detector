# Vision Document

## Why Are We Building This Project?  
We are building the AI Tutoring System to help students overcome common learning challenges such as lack of personalized guidance, difficulty accessing quality study resources, and inefficient study planning. Many students struggle to find tailored support that adapts to their learning pace and style. This project aims to provide an intelligent, user-friendly platform that enhances the learning experience by offering personalized tutoring powered by AI.

## Who Does This Help?  
This project primarily helps students of various levels — including high school, college, and remote learners — who seek better ways to organize their studies, clarify doubts, and receive customized learning assistance. By using this system, students can improve their academic performance, gain confidence, and enjoy a more engaging and effective study process.

## Key Goals  
- Deliver a personalized tutoring experience using AI techniques  
- Provide easy access to quality educational content and resources  
- Support flexible learning tailored to individual student needs  
- Enhance student motivation and self-directed learning through intelligent feedback and recommendations  
- Build a scalable and maintainable platform for future improvements
